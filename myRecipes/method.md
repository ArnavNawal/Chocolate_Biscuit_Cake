Grind the biscuits into a fine powder.
add 1/2 teaspoon baking powder.
Now add milk to make a smoothe paste.
Now add a few drops of rose essence.
Take a cake tin and oil it well so the cake does not stick to it.
Now, pour half of the batter in the cake tin.
add Hersheys to the remaining batter and mix it well.
Now pour this remaining batter into the cake tin.
Spread Hersheys on it as a topping.
add some chocolate peices for extra chocolaty taste.
Bake it 200 degree Celsius for 20 minutes.
Your cake is ready!!
